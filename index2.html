“I am proud to be running for District Court Judge for Elko County. It is my strong belief that this important position is one that should reflect impartial objectivity, dependability, and ethical actions at all times. I have a profound respect and love for the law, and I would do my best to serve you in an ethical and dignified manner.

“This is my promise to the residents of Elko County – to serve this community with fairness, commitment and dedication.”

– David Loreman

David Loreman brings over 24 years of legal experience with a variety of trial experience:

  • Private law practice in Elko County, including trial experience in Criminal Law, Juvenile Law, Family Law, Civil Law and Federal Law
  • Appointed Judge Pro Tem in the State of Utah and a private law practice in Salt Lake City, Utah
  • Prosecutor for the City of Albany in the State of Oregon
  • Judicial Clerk for Circuit Court Judges in Salem, Oregon

David Loreman can bring positive changes to the court:

  • Fair and impartial administration of justice
  • Make timely decisions and cooperate with the legal community to streamline procedures and increase the accessability to the Court.
  • Bring more unity between the Courts and their departments and work to stabilize the staff in Department I
  • Enhance and expand the programs of the Court
  • Expand the Court’s contact with both the youth and adults throughout Elko County
