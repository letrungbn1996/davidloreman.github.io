Community Involvement & Recognitions

Since moving to Elko County in 1991, David has been actively involved in many organizations to help enhance the opportunities for both our community and our youth. He has had the opportunity to serve as a long time member of the Elko County Library Board, a leader with the Boy Scouts of America, a Rotarian, and a referee for all Elko County High Schools.

Community Positions Held:

  • Two time District Chairman for the Overland District for the Boy Scouts of America
  • Past Board Member and Chairman of United Way of the Great Basin
  • Past President and Current Member of the Desert Sunrise Rotary Club
  • Past Board Member and Chairman of the Northeast Nevada Development Authority (NENDA)
